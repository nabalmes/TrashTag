About

Hi! We're

Trash Tag

We are a passionate group of students from LPU-Batangas driven to create a more sustainable future through innovative waste management solutions. Recognizing the challenges of effective waste segregation, we've developed TrashTag: Scan, Sort, Earn Rewards-a user-friendly approach that combines technology and incentives to revolutionize how we handle waste.

Our platform utilizes a website as a central hub for comprehensive information about different types of waste and proper disposal methods. To make this information readily accessible and engaging we've integrated QR codes that can be easily scanned to provide instant guidance.

What sets TrashTag apart is our unique rewards system. We believe that by incentivizing responsible waste management, we can foster a greater sense of community involvement and encourage consistent participation.

Our objective is to enable people, businesses, and institutions (such as manufacturing plants and hospitals) to embrace more ecologically responsible and effective waste management techniques. TrashTag is our contribution to bringing our vision of a future where technology plays a significant part in fostering cleaner, greener communities to life.
